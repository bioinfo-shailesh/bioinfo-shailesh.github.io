About Me

I am a cancer biologist with a strong interest in deciphering the molecular and cellular mechanisms that drive cancer progression, therapy resistance, and tumor–immune interactions. My current research emphasizes racial disparities in cancer and investigates critical signaling pathways, including Notch and the PPARγ–ELF3 axis.

I earned my PhD in Zoology from the prestigious Banaras Hindu University (BHU), India, where I developed a solid foundation in computational biology and insect ecology...
